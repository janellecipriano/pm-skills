## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-22

### Added
- Initial release: 19 open-source Program Management skills for Claude, organized into four groups.
- **Core PM:** `project-charter`, `status-report`, `raid-log`, `roadmap-planning`, `launch-readiness`, `retrospective`, `decision-log`.
- **Discovery & Planning:** `prd-review`, `sprint-planning`, `dependency-mapping`, `estimation-sizing`.
- **Stakeholder Communications:** `exec-summary`, `stakeholder-comms-plan`, `escalation`, `release-notes`.
- **Metrics & Ops:** `okr-tracking`, `postmortem`, `capacity-planning`, `program-health-dashboard`.
- Starter templates for `project-charter`, `status-report`, and `raid-log`.
- Repository docs: `README`, `CONTRIBUTING`, `CODE_OF_CONDUCT`, MIT `LICENSE`, and `.gitignore`.
